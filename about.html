What is Software Compare?

SoftwareCompare aims to solve the problem of easily finding simple and objective data on software online. This site provides tools to aid people in their journey of choosing software that best suits their needs. The charts compare an assortment of criteria such as privacy, usability, etc. which allow users to assess their own needs and discover applications that best meet those needs.
