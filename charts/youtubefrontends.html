YouTube Frontend Comparisons

YouTube Frontends are applications that can be run on-device or on a server. Frontends mimic Youtube's native app or site with more privacy, no ads, and sometimes new features.
*Web frontends on popular instances are prone to breakage, so I recommend using a less popular instance, self hosting or using an application.
